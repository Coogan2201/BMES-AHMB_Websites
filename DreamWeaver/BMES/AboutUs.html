Who we are


The Binghamton Biomedical Engineering Society (BMES), formerly known as the BUBioengineers (BBE) is a club at Binghamton University that seeks to stimulate networking skills between students, faculty, and the community. We strive to promote professional development and provide our members the confidence to be successful at and beyond Binghamton.


What we do

Professional Development:

BMES offers a number of Professional Development events for our members, including: Mock Interviews, Workshops, Lecture Series, and more.

Community Service:

A big part of BBE is giving back to the community. Every year, we host a number of Community Service Events. Past events include: ICK! (I'm a Complex Kid), High School Math & Science Fair, SHARE Mentor Program, Engineers Week, and JC Science Night Out.

Social Activities:

BBE holds at least 1 social event per semester. Past events include: Paintball, Ice Skating, Apple Picking, and Observatory Visits.
