Executive Board

The current Executive Board positions in our chapter include the President, Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer. Duties of these positions are outlined within the chapter constitution. If you are interested in running for a position on our Executive Board, nominations and elections are held at the end of each spring semester. Additional positions may be suggested by the general body at any time and created with a majority vote by the present officials.
