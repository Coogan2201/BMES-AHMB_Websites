42nd Annual NEBEC Conference

The 42nd Annual Northeast Bioengineering Conference on April 5-7, will be hosted by Binghamton University. We are currently looking for student volunteers to help with the setup and logistics of the Conference. You can register here; Attendance to the conference is free for volunteers and preference is given to BME students. More information can be found on the conference webpage. AEMB members who volunteer can fulfill their member requirements by volunteering at the conference. Hope to see you there!
